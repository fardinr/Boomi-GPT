to enable or disable boomi assure, go to account information. about this task you must have an assigned role that includes the account administration or boomi assure privilege. procedure in the settings > account information and setup page, select the account information tab. select enable boomi assure. a confirmation dialog opens. read the notice in the dialog and then click ok. optional: click the read privacy policy link. the privacy policy opens in another browser tab or window. when you are done reviewing the policy, close it. click save.
