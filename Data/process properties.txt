the deployed processes panel displays all deployed processes for the selected atom, molecule, or cloud. after selecting a process, you can edit its process properties in the edit process properties dialog. you may want to change a process's properties or you may need to preset a property to a particular value when you are testing or troubleshooting a process and its resulting documents. you can edit these types of process properties: the date and/or time of the predefined properties com.boomi.execution.lastrun and com.boomi.execution.lastsuccessfulrun. the property values of any process property components that you may have created. see the process property components topic. the property values of any dynamic process properties that you may have created. see the dynamic process properties topic.
