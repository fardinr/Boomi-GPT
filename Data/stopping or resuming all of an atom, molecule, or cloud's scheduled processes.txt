stop or resume all scheduled processes on the deployed processes panel. before you begin you must have the atom management privilege and the scheduling privilege to perform this task. if you have the atom management read access privilege, you cannot perform the task. procedure go to manage > atom management. select the atom, molecule, or cloud from the list on the left. on the deployed processes panel, do one of the following: click stop all schedules and confirm that you want to stop all schedules by clicking ok.a square red icon appears next to each process whose schedule has been stopped. click resume all schedules and confirm that you want to resume all schedules by clicking ok.a green arrow icon appears next to each process whose schedule has been started.
