processes contain a series of connected steps, represented by shapes. connecting two shapes is a drag-and-drop operation, or by clicking on the connecting icon that originates from the source shape. procedure hover over the green arrow originating from the source shape. the green arrow shows a plus sign. click on the plus sign to view and select from the list of available shapes. after connecting the shapes, the arrow turns green. to add additional inline shapes, repeat step 2. note: you can also add a shape by dragging it onto the process canvas from the shapes palette to the left of the canvas.
