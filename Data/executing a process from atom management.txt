manually execute a process from atom management by selecting an atom and a process. before you begin you must have the atom management privilege to perform this task. if you have the atom management read access privilege, you cannot perform the task. procedure go to manage > atom management. on the left side of the page, select the atom to which the process is deployed. the processes deployed to this atom appear on the deployed processes panel. select the process. click the blue arrow next to the process and select execute process. this option is not available for listener processes. go to deploy > processes to see the results.
