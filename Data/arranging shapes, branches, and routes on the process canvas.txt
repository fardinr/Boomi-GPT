with a single click you can automatically arrange the shapes, branches, and routes on the process canvas to make your process easier to understand and maintain. procedure click the arrange link on the process canvas. note: if the process has unsaved changes, you are prompted to either save, don't save, or cancel. when the shapes, branches, and routes are arranged, notes and unconnected shapes are moved to the bottom left. do one of the following: if you are satisfied with the arrangement, click save or save and close. if you are dissatisfied with the arrangement, either make adjustments to the arrangement manually, or click the revert link to revert the process to the last saved revision.
