to view or delete tests from boomi assure, open boomi assure on the manage menu. about this taskboomi assure must already be enabled in the account. you must have the boomi assure privilege, which is part of the standard user role, enabled to view and delete tests from boomi assure. procedure on the manage menu, select boomi assure. the boomi assure page opens, displaying all tests from your account that have been submitted. to remove all tests, do one of the following: click the remove all data button. click the remove your data link. in the confirmation dialog, click ok. to remove one test, do one of the following: click the remove process test data button. click the remove boomi assure data icon. in the confirmation dialog, click ok.
