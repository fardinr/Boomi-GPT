use delete atom on the atom information panel to delete information about an atom, molecule, or cloud molecule from the platform. about this taskan atom, molecule, or cloud molecule that has been deleted or is no longer being used might appear in the list on the atom management and process reporting pages. the cause could be that the machine on which it was installed is no longer functioning or has not yet sent this information to the boomi platform. if this happens, notify the boomi platform that the machine has been deleted. procedure go to manage > atom management. select the atom, molecule, or cloud molecule in the list on the left. in information, click atom information. on the panel on the right, click delete atom. a confirmation dialog opens. click ok.
