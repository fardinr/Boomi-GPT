you can view a previous revision of a component by clicking the view/edit revision icon for that revision in the revision history dialog. this option is not available in the base edition. procedure in the component explorer, navigate to the folder containing the component. double-click the component. the component opens on a tab. click the revision history icon. the revision history dialog opens. click the view/edit revision icon next to a revision number. the older revision of the component opens on a new tab with a banner indicating the revision number. close the older revision of the component. no changes are made.
