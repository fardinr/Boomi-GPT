use the manage > atom management > environments page to attach or detach a role to an environment. before you begin you must have the atom management and environment management privileges to perform this task. if you have the atom management read access privilege, you cannot perform the task. about this task procedure go to manage > atom management. in the environments list on the left, click the environment's name. the environment properties page opens. in the roles with access list, turn on the check boxes for those roles you wish to attach to the selected environment. to detach a role, turn off the check boxes in the roles with access list for those roles you do no longer want to attach to the environment.
