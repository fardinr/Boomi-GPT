upon successful completion, your process needs to send an acknowledgment back to salesforce to close the loop for the outbound message. you can simply copy and paste the following xml snipet into a message shape before the return documents shape in your process. <soapenv:envelope xmlns:soapenv="http:.xmlsoap.org/soap/envelope/"> <soapenv:body> <notificationsresponse xmlns="http://soap.sforce.com/2005/09/outbound"> <ack>true</ack> </notificationsresponse> </soapenv:body> </soapenv:envelope>
