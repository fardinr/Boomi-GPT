set the amount of time before a molecule’s or atom cloud’s node is shut down or restarted. procedure in the manage menu, select atom management. select the molecule or cloud molecule from the list on the left. in settings & configuration, click account properties. click the advanced tab. select the cluster problem automatic shutdown delay property, click add property, and add a whole number, in milliseconds, in the property value field. click save. because changes to this property do not take effect until the molecule or atom cloud is restarted, you are prompted to restart. you have the option to restart now or restart later.
