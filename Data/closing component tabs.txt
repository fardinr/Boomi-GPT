you can close individual component tabs opened on the integration build page, close all component tabs simultaneously, or close all tabs other than the component you are currently working on. procedure to close a single process or component tab in the build page, click the close button on the tab you wish to close. you are prompted to save any unsaved changes before the process or component is closed. to close all opened processes or components in the build page, click the (actions menu) in the upper right hand corner above the process canvas, and select close all from the list of options. you are prompted to save any unsaved changes before components are closed. to close all opened processes or components in the build page other than the tab currently in focus, click the (actions menu) in the upper right hand corner above the process canvas, and select close other components from the list of options. you are prompted to save any unsaved changes before components are closed.
