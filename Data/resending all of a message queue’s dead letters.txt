resend a message queue’s dead letters from the actions menu on the queue management panel of atom management. before you begin you must have the atom management privilege to perform this task. if you have the atom management read access privilege, you cannot perform the task. procedure on the manage menu select atom management. select the atom, molecule, or cloud from the list on the left. in runtime, click queue management. the queue management panel opens. if the dead letters you are resending are associated with a subscriber queue, in the actions menu for the applicable publish/subscribe queue, select manage subscriber queues. in the actions menu for the message queue, select view dead letters. the message queue’s dead letter list appears. click retry and select all. a confirmation dialog opens. click ok.
