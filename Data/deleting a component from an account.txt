not available. if the component is not referenced by other components, a confirmation window appears and you can skip to the last step of this task. if the component is referenced by other components, the confirm deletion of referenced component dialog opens. optional: if you want to view a component that references the component that you want to delete: in the dialog, navigate to the folder
