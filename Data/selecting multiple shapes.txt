you can simultaneously select multiple shapes for moving or removal by dragging a rectangle around them. procedure click and drag a rectangle around the shapes that you want to select. a transparent rectangle appears on the canvas. a button bar displaying the number of selected shapes appears. optional: to move the selected shapes, click one of the shapes within the selection rectangle and drag it to the new location. all selected shapes move to the new location. optional: to remove the selected shapes, click remove, then click ok. the shapes are removed; you cannot undo this action.
