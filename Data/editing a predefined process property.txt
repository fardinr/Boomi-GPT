use the deployed processes panel to edit a predefined process property. before you begin you must have the atom management privilege to perform this task. if you have the atom management read access privilege, you cannot perform the task. procedure go to manage > atom management. select the atom, molecule, or cloud from the list on the left. on the deployed processes panel, select the process and click the blue arrow next to it. select edit process properties. the edit process properties dialog opens. change the date of the com.boomi.execution.lastrun and/or com.boomi.execution.lastsuccessfulrun property by clicking in the date field and selecting a date from the calendar. change the time of the com.boomi.execution.lastrun and/or com.boomi.execution.lastsuccessfulrun property by clicking the arrows and selecting an hour, minute and second from the lists. click save.
